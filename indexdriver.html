Traffic Signals and Symbols

No Parking

No parking signs give customers and others clear notice about spots to avoid leaving their vehicles. It is place were parking not allow.

U-Turn prohibited

The U turn at some intersections could result in major accidents or traffic jams. At these place this sign is installed which prohibit overtaking ensuring safety.

Stop

The stop sign is placed at crossroads, when a road doesn't have traffic lights. This sign says drivers to stop when reach crossroad and let other drivers to go.

No Stopping or Standing

A no standing sign means that the driver cannot leave the car, and a no stopping sign means stopping is only allowed to obey a traffic sign, signal or officer.

Left turn Prohibited

This sign directs driver not to turn towards right side in any circumstance. This sign directs driver not to turn towards left side in any circumstance.

Speed Limit

Road speed limits are used to set the legal maximum or minimum speed at which road vehicles may travel on a given stretch of road.
